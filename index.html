bio

747 is a Canadian DJ and producer known for his emotive acid-tinged works. His music is a constant evolution with a mixture of lush pads, dreamy soundscapes, and the sound of the 303. Inspired by the sounds of Berlin, Detroit, and the acid legends, his sound is high-fidelity yet old-school, uplifting yet melancholic, a journey with no destination.

His breakthrough came with the widely acclaimed Aurora Centralis EP, which quickly established 747 as a leading voice in the acid techno scene. The title track became a global favorite, captivating floors around the world with its emotional depth. He followed up with the eight-track Paleo series on Aquaregia, an ambitious record that further defined his sound and built a dedicated following around his melancholic take on acid. In 2019, his first full-length album The Gate of Life marked a shift toward 90s-inspired trance, introducing a whimsical tone to the often more serious atmosphere of his earlier work.

2025 saw the release of his second full-length studio album, Pacific Spirit, an ode to his hometown of Vancouver in the Pacific Northwest. The album represented a distinct departure from his techno sound of the past decade, while continuing the sonic narrative he's developed over the years, fusing jungle breaks with the sounds of 90s trance and the 303.
